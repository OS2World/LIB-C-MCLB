Description of samples:
-----------------------

SAMP1:
======
A simple example of the multi-column listbox.  Just shows how
to create a MCLB control and insert text into it.

SAMP2:
======
An owner-drawn multi-column listbox.

SAMP3:
======
A simple example of a direct-manipluation listbox with
drag/drop reorder and context menu.

SAMP4:
======
A multicolumn listbox with drag/drop reorder.

SAMP5:
======
A pair of drag/drop listboxes that allow drag/drop
moving and copying of items between them.

SAMP6:
======
A multicolumn listbox with fixed-width columns.
